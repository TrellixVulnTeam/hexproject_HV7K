  •        Withdraw Pounds

    Register your Pound withdrawal bank details to place withdrawal request.

    We process Pound withdrawals during business hours with cut-off time at 1:00PM. Funds are send via International Wire Transfer in Pound and take 1 to 5 business days to arrive in your account. If you register a non-Pound denominatedaccount, funds will be converted at your bank`s rate.

    We charge a flat withdrawal fee of 10 Pound to cover bank charges for processing International Transfer. Please note that your bank may charge you additional fees for accepting an international transfer. The minimum withdrawal amount is 15 Pound.

    To place Pound withdrawal requests you need to register your bank account details for International Transfer(BIC and IBAN). The account you register must be held in your name. Withdrawals to third party accounts are forbidden and will not be processed.

    Daghub holds 100% of customer-owned Nano in multi-signature cold stoarage secured in underground vaults. We process cold stoarage withdrawals 2 to 5 times a week, during business hours.

    Theres is a nominal charge of 0.5 Nano for Nano withdrawals to cover miner fees. The minimum withdrawal amount is 0.5 Nano.

    You are able to registerr one Nano withdrawal adress at a atime. You must be in control of that adress. Please only withdraw Nano to your own wallet. Withdrawals to third party addresses are forbidden and you can not make payments directly from your Daghub account. If you need to use Nano for a payment, please withdraw them from your Nano account tot your wallet first. Daghub monitors Nano transactions and has zero tolerance policy for activity associated with darknet markets . Third party withdrawals and association with dark markets will result in your accounts closure.

    Upon registering the address, you can choose whether you wish to be able to change your withdrawal address anytime, using your Authy token to authenticate the change from website, or lock down your withdrawal address with no option to modify it from the website without contacting Daghub Support and verifying your identity again. After making a change, the removed address will not be available for use againin any Daghub account.
